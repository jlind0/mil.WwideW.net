Mission Statement

*STARFLEET* is dedicated to achieving Roddenberry's vision in the here and now, skipping the devastating World War III whose aftermath brings together Earth in Star Trek. To accomplish this *STARFLEET* officers use cyberwarfare tactics to influence all levels of society toward a peaceful, united front. Our preferred methods of cyberwarfare go beyond the "bits and bytes" to use information as a weapon against tyranny and evil; what can be refereed to as "social engineering".
